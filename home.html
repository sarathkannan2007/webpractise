About LTS

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish consisting of a small, hand-sized corn or wheat tortilla topped with a filling, which is then folded or rolled to be eaten by hand. Believed to have originated in the silver mines of Mexico in the 18th century, the term "taco" originally referred to the small explosive charges used to excavate ore, later adapting to describe the "plug" of food inside a tortilla. Authentic tacos generally feature soft tortillas filled with grilled steak (carne asada), stewed pork (carnitas), or marinated pork (al pastor), garnished simply with onion, cilantro, and lime
